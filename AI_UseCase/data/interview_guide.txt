INTERVIEW PREPARATION GUIDE

1. TELL ME ABOUT YOURSELF (STRUCTURE)
- Start with a short professional summary (1–2 lines).
- Mention 2–3 key achievements that show your strengths.
- Connect your past experience to the job you’re applying for.
- End with what you want next in your career.
- Keep it concise (45–60 seconds).

2. STAR FORMAT FOR ANSWERING QUESTIONS
S – Situation: Provide context.
T – Task: Describe the responsibility you had.
A – Action: Explain what actions you took.
R – Result: Share the measurable outcome.

3. COMMON BEHAVIORAL INTERVIEW QUESTIONS
- Tell me about a time you handled a difficult situation.
- Describe a time you worked under pressure.
- Tell me about a conflict with a team member and how you resolved it.
- Describe a situation where you led a team.
- Give an example of a time you made a mistake and how you corrected it.

4. BEHAVIORAL ANSWER TIPS
- Avoid vague answers.
- Use measurable outcomes when possible.
- Always end with what you learned.

5. TECHNICAL INTERVIEW PREPARATION — PYTHON
- Understand lists, dictionaries, tuples.
- Know OOP principles: classes, inheritance, polymorphism.
- Understand error handling and decorators.
- Be familiar with list comprehensions and generator expressions.

6. SQL INTERVIEW PREPARATION
- SELECT, INSERT, UPDATE basics.
- INNER JOIN, LEFT JOIN, RIGHT JOIN.
- GROUP BY and HAVING clauses.
- Window functions: ROW_NUMBER, RANK, DENSE_RANK.
- Indexing and query optimization basics.

7. DEVOPS & MLOPS (BASIC EXPECTATIONS)
- CI/CD pipeline stages.
- Docker fundamentals: Dockerfile, images, containers.
- Kubernetes basics: pods, deployments, services.
- MLOps concepts: model versioning, model registry, deployment strategies.

8. HR INTERVIEW QUESTIONS AND TIPS
Why should we hire you?
- Match your skills to job requirements.
- Mention past impact and future potential.

What are your strengths?
- Choose strengths relevant to the role.
- Provide real workplace examples.

What are your weaknesses?
- Choose a real weakness.
- Show steps you're taking to improve.

Why do you want this job?
- Mention company culture, role alignment, mission.
- Connect your goals with the job's responsibilities.

9. SALARY NEGOTIATION TIPS
- Always research market compensation.
- Give a salary range, not an exact figure.
- Focus on value you bring, not on numbers alone.

10. INTERVIEW MISTAKES TO AVOID
- Giving long, unstructured answers.
- Speaking negatively about past employers.
- Not preparing questions for the interviewer.
- Interrupting the interviewer.
- Not backing your answers with examples.

11. QUESTIONS TO ASK INTERVIEWER
- What does success look like in this role?
- How is performance measured?
- What are the next steps in the hiring process?
- How would you describe the team culture?
